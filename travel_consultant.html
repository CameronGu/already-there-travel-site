Corporate Travel Consultant Position

Position: Corporate Travel Consultant

The Corporate Travel Consultant is a key role in our core accounts group. They are detail-oriented and focused on finding solutions to client needs. Their responsibilities include making travel and meeting arrangements in an expedient and efficient manner, calling hotels, gaining quotes and negotiating contracts. They have excellent oral and written communication skills (professional native-level English) and are able to organize their work using tools like Google Docs. They will handle direct interaction with clients and parties to transactions all over the world once they have been trained in our proprietary systems.

How To Apply

We review applications in batches, so don't worry if you don't hear from us right away. Qualified candidates will move on to video interviews to discuss the position in detail, meet the team, and talk through the fit.
